Guilty Gear Strive

Guilty Gear Strive is a fighting game developed by Arc System Works and released in June 2021. It is the latest installment in the Guilty Gear series, which has been around since 1998.

The game features a roster of 15 unique characters, each with their own fighting style and special moves. Players can engage in one-on-one battles against the computer or other players in online multiplayer mode.

The game's story mode follows the events of the previous game, Guilty Gear Xrd, as the world faces a threat from an organization known as the Conclave. The story features a mix of 2D anime-style cutscenes and 3D in-game graphics.

Overall, Guilty Gear Strive has been well-received by both critics and fans of the series, with praise given for its stunning graphics, engaging story mode, and challenging gameplay.
